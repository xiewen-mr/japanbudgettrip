Price notes

How we use Japan trip prices and official sources.

Japan Budget Trip uses planning ranges, not live quotes. Prices on the site are meant to help travelers compare decisions before booking: hotel location, route shape, airport transfers, JR Pass value, food budget, mobile data, and insurance basics.

Why not use live scraped hotel prices?

Hotel and OTA prices change constantly, may depend on cookies, region, occupancy, cancellation policy, taxes, and platform terms. Instead of scraping live hotel listings, this site uses planning ranges and points readers toward the decision that matters: stay area, station access, room type, cancellation terms, and total route cost.
