About

Independent Japan trip planning for travelers who care about the numbers.

Japan Budget Trip publishes practical planning guides for first-time and budget-conscious travelers visiting Japan. The site focuses on trip costs, Tokyo routes, transport decisions, hotel areas, mobile data, insurance, and other booking choices that affect the final budget.

How guides are written

Guides are written to help readers compare options before booking. We prioritize clear ranges, realistic tradeoffs, and decisions that a traveler can act on. Prices, pass rules, transport products, and provider policies can change, so readers should verify final prices and terms directly with official providers before purchasing.

Editorial approach

Our goal is to explain what matters, where money is usually wasted, and how to build a Japan route that fits the traveler rather than a generic checklist. We avoid pretending that one route, pass, hotel area, or data plan is best for everyone.
